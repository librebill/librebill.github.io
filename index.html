LibreBill

A new way to do billing.

LibreBill is a free and open source billing platform solution for web hosts, cloud providers and freelancers.

Our mission is to build a billing platform that is extendable, themable and API-centric so it can be used by anyone. From freelancers that bill by the hour to cloud providers that hate being hostaged by the price increases of their billing platform — we got you.

LibreBill is under active development and is sponsored by Dual, a software development and cloud consulting firm based in Manila. We are a team of engineers that hated the vendor lock-in of leading solutions and decided to build a solution that works for everyone.

LibreBill is being built with Laravel and Vue and is planned to be released under the GNU General Public License v3.0.
